# Business Models for Online Table Tennis Equipment

## Model Comparison

| Model | Startup Cost | Margin | Risk | Time to Revenue |
|-------|-------------|--------|------|-----------------|
| Dropship (AliExpress) | $0 | 20–40% | Low | 1 week |
| Curated Importer | $500–$2K | 55–70% | Medium | 2–4 weeks |
| Private Label (OEM) | $1K–$5K | 70–85% | Medium-High | 2–3 months |
| Authorized Dealer | $10K–$50K | 30–45% | High | 1–3 months |

## Model 1: Dropship from AliExpress

**How it works**: Customer orders on your site → you order from AliExpress seller → ships directly to customer

**Pros**:
- Zero inventory investment
- Test unlimited products
- No shipping/warehouse hassle

**Cons**:
- 7–15 day shipping (customers expect 2–3 days)
- No quality control
- Can't brand or customize packaging
- Margins thin after ads/fees
- Return handling is nightmare (ship back to China?)

**Verdict**: Only viable as initial demand validation, not long-term model

## Model 2: Curated Importer (RECOMMENDED FOR PINGPRO)

**How it works**: Buy proven bestsellers from Chinese brands in small batches (10–50), ship to US, sell with expert curation

**Pros**:
- Strong margins (55–70%)
- Fast US shipping (competitive advantage over AliExpress)
- Quality control (inspect before selling)
- Build brand as "curator of best Chinese TT equipment"
- Low MOQ — many brands sell wholesale at 20+ pieces
- Yinhe/Sanwei/LOKI are hungry for US distribution

**Cons**:
- Need $500–$2K upfront inventory
- Storage space needed (but blades are small — 50 fit in a shoebox)
- Must handle returns/exchanges

**Why this works for PingPro**:
- Fills a real market gap (Yinhe NOT available in US retail)
- Site already positions as premium/expert
- Contact form already segments by "blade recommendation" intent
- "Pro Blades from $89" pricing aligns perfectly with this model

**Execution**:
1. Order 5–10 blades from AliExpress (sample quality, photograph)
2. List on site with real photos + expert review
3. Take orders via Stripe
4. Initially fulfill from AliExpress (accept 10-day shipping)
5. After 10+ sales: contact brand direct, buy wholesale, ship from home

## Model 3: Private Label / Own Brand

**How it works**: Design a blade spec → OEM factory produces with your branding → sell as PingPro-branded equipment

**Pros**:
- Highest margins (70–85%)
- Complete brand control
- No competition on same product
- ITTF doesn't require blade approval (only rubber needs it)
- MOQ as low as 50 pieces from some factories

**Cons**:
- Need blade design knowledge (or hire consultant)
- Sample iteration takes time (2–4 rounds typical)
- Risk of producing something that doesn't play well
- Need to build brand trust from zero
- Initial investment $1K–$5K

**Typical OEM Process**:
1. Contact factory on Alibaba (Hangzhou Wellssport, Yangzhou Haitian)
2. Specify: ply count, wood types, composite layers, handle shape, weight target
3. Factory sends 2–3 samples ($50–$100 for samples + express shipping)
4. Test, give feedback, iterate
5. Approve final sample
6. Production run: 50–100 pieces
7. Receive, photograph, list

**Blade Design Ideas for PingPro First Product**:
- "PingPro V1" — 5W+2C (limba/ayous/carbon), ~85g, FL handle
- Target: intermediate players upgrading from premade racket
- Comparable to Butterfly Viscaria but at $65–$80
- Cost from factory: $6–$10/piece

## Model 4: Content + Affiliate (Low Risk Complement)

**How it works**: Create valuable content (reviews, guides, comparisons) → link to retailers → earn commission

**Revenue sources**:
- Amazon Associates: 4–8% on TT equipment
- Megaspin/TT11 affiliate programs: 5–10%
- YouTube ad revenue (if doing video)
- Sponsored reviews from brands

**Content that drives TT equipment sales**:
- "Best blades for [playing style]" articles
- Head-to-head blade comparisons with data
- "Budget vs Premium: Can you tell the difference?" videos
- Blade finder quizzes
- Player interview/setup reveal content

**Verdict**: Best combined with Model 2 — content drives traffic → some buy from you directly, rest earn affiliate

## Model 5: Hybrid (OPTIMAL for PingPro)

**Combine**:
- Model 2 (curated import) for core revenue — Yinhe, Sanwei blades with 60%+ margin
- Model 4 (content) for traffic and authority — reviews, comparisons, guides
- Model 3 (private label) as Phase 3 — one signature blade design

**Revenue Mix Target (Month 6)**:
- Direct blade sales: 70% of revenue
- Affiliate commissions: 15%
- Accessories/rubber sales: 15%

|-------------|----------|

| Resellers | Counterfeit risk; inconsistent quality; no returns |
