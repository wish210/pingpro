## Pricing Strategy for PingPro

### Sweet Spot Positioning
```
AliExpress direct:  $20–$50  (7-15 day shipping, no support, questionable returns)
     |
PingPro target:     $50–$90  (US warehouse, 2-day ship, expert advice, returns)
     |
Japanese brands:    $150–$250 (similar performance at 3x cost)
```

### Why Customers Pay More Than AliExpress
1. **Shipping speed**: 2-day vs 7-15 days
2. **Trust**: US-based returns/support vs Chinese seller disputes
3. **Curation**: Expert recommendation vs overwhelming AliExpress catalog
4. **Authenticity guarantee**: No counterfeit risk
5. **Blade selection advice**: Personalized (quiz/chat)
