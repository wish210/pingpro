# Supply Chain & Sourcing Guide

## Sourcing Channels (Ranked by Viability for Startup)

### 1. AliExpress (Lowest Risk Start)
- **Use for**: Initial samples, testing demand, first 10–50 sales
- **Price**: Retail-direct from Chinese brands (Yinhe, Sanwei, LOKI, Stuor)
- **MOQ**: 1 piece
- **Shipping to US**: 7–15 days (standard), 3–7 days (express ~$5–$10/item)
- **Payment**: Credit card, PayPal
- **Risk**: Slow shipping for end customers if dropshipping
- **Strategy**: Buy 5–10 of bestsellers, ship from home/warehouse

### 2. Direct from Brand (Best for Scaling)
- **Use for**: After validating demand (10+ sales of a model)
- **Brands with English export departments**: Yinhe, Sanwei, DHS, LOKI
- **Price**: 30–50% below AliExpress retail
- **MOQ**: Usually 20–50 pieces per model
- **Shipping**: Sea freight (3–5 weeks) or air express (5–7 days)
- **Payment**: Wire transfer, sometimes PayPal/Alibaba Trade Assurance
- **How to contact**: 
  - Yinhe: sales@yinhe-tt.com (or find on Alibaba: "Shijiazhuang Galaxy Sports")
  - Sanwei: export@sanwei.com.cn
  - LOKI: via Alibaba storefront "Loki Sports Goods Co., Ltd"
  
### 3. Alibaba / Made-in-China (For OEM/Private Label)
- **Use for**: Creating your own brand blade
- **Key factories**:
  - Hangzhou Wellssport Manufactory (Zhejiang) — $3.80–$15, MOQ 100–500
  - Telamon Sports Goods (Shanghai) — $2.80–$10, MOQ 50, Diamond Member
  - Shenyang Dawn Star (Liaoning) — $8.70–$13, MOQ 300, rosewood/carbon
  - Loki Sports Goods (Yangzhou, Jiangsu) — also does OEM
  - Yangzhou Haitian Athletic Equipment (Jiangsu) — $3.36–$13.51, 2-7 ply
- **Customization available**: Logo engraving, custom handle shape, blade design, packaging, wood selection
- **Timeline**: Sample (2–3 weeks) → approval → production (3–5 weeks)
- **Cost for first private-label run**: ~$500–$1,500 (50–100 blades)

### 4. Taobao / 1688.com (Cheapest, Most Complex)
- **Use for**: Sourcing unique models not on AliExpress, or max cost savings
- **Price**: 20–40% below AliExpress (domestic Chinese pricing)
- **Challenge**: Chinese language, domestic payment (Alipay), no direct international shipping
- **Solution**: Use a Taobao agent (Superbuy, WeGoBuy, CSSBuy) — adds ~10% fee + shipping
- **Best for**: Buying DHS Hurricane blades at domestic price, limited edition models

## Blade Construction Knowledge (For Informed Sourcing)

### Wood Types (Face/Outer Ply)
| Wood | Origin | Character | Cost |
|------|--------|-----------|------|
| Limba | Africa | Soft, good feel, classic | Medium |
| Koto | Japan/SE Asia | Hard, fast, crisp | Medium-High |
| Hinoki | Japan (cypress) | Premium feel, power, resonance | Very High |
| Ayous | Africa | Light, flexible, good dwell | Low-Medium |
| Rosewood | SE Asia | Dense, hard, fast | High |
| Walnut | Global | Balanced, stable | Medium |

### Core Woods
| Wood | Character | Use |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Ayous | Light, soft | Speed amplification |
| Balsa | Ultra-light | Weight reduction (Yinhe T-series uses this) |
| Kiri | Very light | Japanese light blades |
| Spruce | Stiff, resonant | All-wood offensive blades |

### Composite Layers
| Material | Brand Name | Character | Who Uses It |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Arylate-Carbon (ALC) | Butterfly patent term | Balanced speed + control | Butterfly, copied widely |
| Zylon-Carbon (ZLC) | Butterfly patent term | Maximum speed, less control | Butterfly only (genuine) |
| Super ZLC | Butterfly | Extreme speed | Butterfly Timo Boll line |
| LD Carbon | Generic | Similar to ALC feel | Sanwei, Yinhe |
| Glass fiber | Generic | Adds stiffness subtly | Budget carbon-like blades |
| Aramid-Carbon | Generic ALC equivalent | Same as ALC, different name | Yinhe V14, Sanwei 75 |

### Ply Configurations
- **5W** (5-ply all-wood): Classic feel, control-oriented, flexible. Best for: loops, feeling.
- **7W** (7-ply all-wood): Faster, stiffer, direct. Best for: flat hits, speed.
- **5W+2C** (5 wood + 2 carbon/composite): Most popular modern config. Speed + feel balance.
- **3W+2C** (inner carbon, fewer wood plies): Even faster, less dwell time.
- **7W+2C** (7 wood + 2 carbon): Very fast, stiff, for smashers.

## Quality Indicators (What to Check in Samples)

1. **Handle finish**: Smooth, no rough spots, comfortable grip
2. **Edge gluing**: Clean, no visible gaps between plies
3. **Weight consistency**: Variation should be <3g between same model
4. **Surface flatness**: Lay on glass — should be perfectly flat
5. **Bounce test**: Drop ball from 30cm — bounce should be uniform across surface
6. **Resonance**: Tap blade — clear ring = good construction, dull thud = possible delamination
7. **Carbon layer alignment**: Visible through translucent outer ply — should be even weave

## Shipping & Logistics

### From China to US
| Method | Time | Cost (per blade) | Best For |
|--------|------|------------------|----------|
| ePacket/China Post | 10–20 days | $3–$5 | Samples, small orders |
| AliExpress Standard | 7–15 days | $5–$8 | Small batches |
| Air Express (DHL/FedEx) | 3–7 days | $15–$25 | Urgent restocks |
| Sea freight | 25–40 days | $1–$2 (bulk) | 100+ pieces |

### Customs / Import
- HS code for table tennis blades: 9506.40 (table tennis articles)
- US import duty: 0% (most TT equipment is duty-free under harmonized tariff)
- No special certifications required for blades (unlike rubbers with chemical regulations)
- FDA not applicable (not food/drug/cosmetic)

## Minimum Viable Inventory Plan

### Phase 1 — Test ($100–$200)
- 2x Yinhe V14 Pro ($45 each)
- 2x Sanwei FEXTRA 7 ($22 each)  
- 2x LOKI K5 Pro ($20 each)
- Total: ~$175 for 6 blades (3 models, 2 each)
- Sell at $70–$90 each = $420–$540 revenue

### Phase 2 — Validate ($500–$1,000)
- Buy 10 each of top 3 sellers from Phase 1
- Contact brand directly for wholesale pricing (expect 30–40% discount)
- Cost: ~$15–$35 per blade at wholesale
- Total: 30 blades @ $25 avg = $750

### Phase 3 — Scale ($2,000–$5,000)
- Establish distributor relationship with 1–2 brands
- Stock 50–100 blades across 8–12 models
- Consider one private-label design via OEM factory
- Add rubbers (same supply chain, higher margin)
